Travel Tips

MakeMyTrip

Make my trip is a website that you can book online for tours, travel, hotels, flight or holiday packages. It is one of the most popular websites and will offer many discount coupons and has great customer service for changes in plans.

Oyo Rooms

OYO rooms: Oyo is a network of budget hotels in India. If applicable or available in the place you are visiting you can get very cheap rooms in big chain hotels or motels in India but with limited features that may be available with rooms at the regular rate at the hotel

Indigo Airlines

Trendy young low cost airline. Food is not included but you can purchase. They are always the best with time and punctuality. Most reliable in terms of getting you from place a to B with consistent quality.

Spicejet

This is probably the most low cost airline. However this airline will tend to have some hidden costs and does not include any food. Not all airlines have even snacks being offered. The seats are also a little more crammed for foot space especially if you are a tall person.

Jet Airways

This is good quality airlines and will provide a meal when you travel. If booked in advance you can still get good deals on their flights. This is a good option for especially longer flight if you are flying across the country.

Uber/Ola

For internal travel, all big cities in India have Uber. Uber in most cities will let you book a cab as well as auto rickshaws. Some cities have share ride and shuttles as well further making travel cheap and convenient . Ola is a top competitor of Uber and provides all services that Uber does. It is an app that you can download like Uber and tends to some time offer competitively lower pricing than Uber
